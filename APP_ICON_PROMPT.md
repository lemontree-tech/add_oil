# App Icon Design Prompt for "加油"

Create a modern, elegant app icon for a daily motivational quote app called "加油" (pronounced "gaa1 jau2", meaning "add oil" or "keep going" in Cantonese).

## App Context:
- **App Name**: 加油 (Add Oil)
- **Purpose**: Daily motivational quotes app for Hong Kongers
- **Target Audience**: Young people aged 10-30 in Hong Kong
- **Theme**: Motivational, educational, encouraging perseverance
- **Design Style**: Modern, elegant, peaceful, youthful
- **UI Theme**: Features a dramatic blackhole/space background with elegant quote cards

## Icon Requirements:

**Style:**
- Modern, minimalist design that works at small sizes (app icon)
- Elegant and sophisticated, yet approachable
- Should convey energy, motivation, and inspiration
- Clean, simple design that's recognizable at 16x16px to 1024x1024px

**Visual Elements (choose one or combine):**
- Option 1: Abstract representation of "加油" - could be stylized Chinese characters or a modern interpretation
- Option 2: Symbol representing motivation/energy - like a rising sun, upward arrow, or energy wave
- Option 3: Minimalist quote/document icon - representing daily wisdom
- Option 4: Space/cosmic theme - subtle reference to the blackhole background (but keep it simple for icon)

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep space colors (dark navy, black) with accent colors
- Accent: Warm, energetic colors (amber, gold, cyan, or purple) for contrast
- Should work on both light and dark backgrounds
- Consider gradient from dark to light (representing dawn/new day)

**Design Principles:**
- Must be readable and recognizable at very small sizes
- Avoid fine details that disappear when scaled down
- Use bold, clear shapes
- High contrast for visibility
- Should feel premium and polished

**Cultural Considerations:**
- Should resonate with Hong Kong youth culture
- Modern and international feel, not overly traditional
- Can subtly reference Chinese culture but keep it contemporary

**Technical Specs:**
- Square format (1:1 aspect ratio)
- Should work as both iOS and Android app icon
- Consider rounded corners (iOS) and square (Android) versions
- High resolution (1024x1024px minimum)

**Mood/Feeling:**
- Inspiring and uplifting
- Peaceful yet energetic
- Modern and fresh
- Premium quality
- Approachable and friendly

## Example Direction:
A minimalist icon featuring a stylized upward energy wave or rising element in deep space colors (navy/black) with a warm accent color (amber/gold/cyan), representing daily motivation and the "add oil" spirit. The design should be simple enough to work at tiny sizes but elegant enough to feel premium.
